What We Do

Who are we


BabesGotBytes is a group of women who are passionate about technology and willing to give back to the community by empowering girls to code.

Vision


All South African primary, high school and out of community girls in marginalised communities to have sufficient access to science, technology, engineering and mathematics (STEM) laboratories, STEM resources, STEM learning support materials and STEM interventions programmes in order for learners to excel in these fields.

Mission


To encourage, motivate and support girls in South African primary, high schools and out of community youth in marginalised communities to study technology, engineering subjects and to pursue STEM course and careers.

Why we do it?

We believe educating girls is the key to changing the world, with 23,000 unfilled software development jobs in the Western Cape alone.

Women currently in South Africa hold 19% in tech industry and is one of the highest in the world. The steady increase of the youth population is not being matched by an increase in job opportunities. There is a large discrepancy between what the youth are being taught and what employers expect them to know. BabesGotBytes NPC has developed a powerful, impactful model. We have a bold vision of turning impoverished communities offering very few opportunities and hope into dynamic and vibrant tech hubs which can have a national and global impact.
